## ✍️ Writing Philosophy

> These blogs are written the way I wish someone had explained these topics to me — with real examples, clear trade-offs, and a "so what does this mean for my product?" lens baked in at every step.

Each post aims to:
- ✅ Explain the concept clearly (no assumed CS background)
- ✅ Show real-world product implications
- ✅ Give you a mental model you can use in your next sprint or stakeholder meeting
- ✅ Be honest about trade-offs — not just hype
